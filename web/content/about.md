# About SpecAtlas

SpecAtlas is a reference for technical specifications and standards. The current focus is building the content around
Digital Identity technologies. The atlas is in the very early stages of development and the current implementation should
be seen as a step beyond a proof of concept. As such, there are numerous opportunities for changes, enhancements, and
improvements.

## Project Goals

SpecAtlas is designed to become a comprehensive reference to technical specifications including connections across
specifications, expert-level commentary on the design of specifications, and implementation guidance.
